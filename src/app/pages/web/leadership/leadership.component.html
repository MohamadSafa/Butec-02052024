Message of the Founder

BUTEC, established over 50 years ago, has overcome challenges such as Lebanon’s civil war and current regional turmoil. Passing the leadership to Chairman Ziad Younes, I am confident in the company’s continued success. My focus now turns to Lebanon’s societal unity and the pursuit of a democratic civil state. Despite the difficulty of leaving my cherished career, I am compelled to contribute to national issues. I encourage your commitment to values and ethics, emphasising the significance of your achievements in Lebanon’s struggle. I remain devoted to your endeavours and the vision of an “Other Lebanon.”
Dr. Nizar Younes
Founder

Message of the Chairman

BUTEC, or “Bureau Technique d’Etudes et de Construction,” began as an innovative EPC contractor over 50 years ago. The company values the potential of its workforce, emphasizing innovation and improvement. Committed to fulfilling contractual obligations globally, BUTEC combines the cost structure of a local contractor with the technical expertise of an international one. Despite its growth, the company has preserved a family- oriented culture. As chairman since 2015, I am proud of our achievements and look forward to the next phase of growth. In these uncertain times, preserving BUTEC’s tradition of dynamic yet careful movement is crucial for continued success. I rely on each team member to contribute to the exciting journey ahead.
Ziad Younes
Chairman

Our Commitments

Integrated Management System

BUTEC’s Integrated Management System (IMS) consolidates quality, environment, and occupational health and safety management systems, streamlining processes for increased efficiency. The IMS guides the company’s activities, ensuring the necessary procedures and documentation align with BUTEC’s certifications in Quality, Environment, and Occupational Health and Safety.

Quality, Health, Safety, Environment

BUTEC attributes its success and growth to a strong emphasis on quality in all activities. The company is dedicated to environmental protection on project sites and prioritizes workforce health and safety by consistently allocating resources to mitigate risks. Despite diverse project requirements, BUTEC adapts effectively, as seen in its numerous certifications. These achievements align with sustainable growth practices.

Diversity

BUTEC fosters a growth-oriented, inclusive work environment that promotes personal and career success. Diversity is a central corporate initiative, emphasizing recruitment and retention from diverse cultures and backgrounds. We value and nurture our people, ensuring equal opportunities, dignity, and respect for all. BUTEC’s focus on diversity shapes a culture where employees are inspired to share their passion and ideas, contributing to success in our industry.

Sustainability

BUTEC frequently monitors its processes and operations in order to ensure that we are always adhering to health, safety, environmental, and quality standards as well as regulatory changes. We believe in following sustainable business practices by setting high health and safety standards, improving the environment, and achieving high client satisfaction while operating in accordance with the acquired ISO standards.
