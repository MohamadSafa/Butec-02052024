PROJECTS
Ain Tsila Development Project

BUTEC contributed to the construction of the 2nd Lebanese biggest city Waste Water Treatment Plant “Tripoli”. This plant financed by the European Investment Bank (EIB) will treat more than 1 000 000 inhabitant equivalent waste water (135 000 m3/day) before discharging the purified water to the Mediterranean Sea. The Plant using the biological treatment processes developed by the SUEZ will treat also the sludge. Indeed, before being incinerated in the plant constructed furnace, the sludge Aerobic Digestion will be used to produce Green Energy to recover part of the facilities electrical consumptions. BUTEC’s scope of work encompassed the Engineering, procurement and construction for civil works and electromechanical networks (process & domestic) as well as installation of process equipment (pretreatment, aeration tanks, clarifiers, digesters, blowers …) of the plant.
